Neptune

Neptune is the fourth and farthest gas giant in our Solar System. It was the first planet located using math. Like Uranus, it's an icy gas giant (coldest in our Solar System). Like all gas giants it has a solid, compressed core.

Planet parametres:

  1. Radius - 24622 kilometers
  2. Distance from the Sun - 30 astronomical units(AU)
  3. Planet mass - 17 Earth masses
  4. Day length - 16 Earth hours
  5. Year length - 165 Earth years
  6. Surface temperature - -200 degrees Celsius
  7. Moon count - 16
  8. G pull - 11.15 m/s2
