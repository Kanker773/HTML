Uranus

Uranus is notorious for its distinct pale cyan color seen from afar(caused by high concentration of methane and ammonia in its atmosphere). Like Jupiter and Saturn beforehand, Uranus is a gas giant with a solid, compressed core. Its unique features - like Neptune, it's an icy gas giant, it spins sideways due to its unique axial tilt (97.77' deg.) and it rotates in the opposite direction (like Venus).

Planet parametres:

  1. Radius - 51118 kilometers
  2. Distance from the Sun - 19 astronomical units(AU)
  3. Planet mass - 14.53 Earth masses
  4. Day length - 17.25 Earth hours
  5. Year length - 84 Earth years
  6. Surface temperature - -195 degrees Celsius
  7. Moon count - 28 (in 13 rings)
  8. G pull - 8.69 m/s2
