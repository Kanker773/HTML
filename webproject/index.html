Jupiter

A magnificent planet with a massive gravity pull that keeps most bigger and smaller asteroids from flying into the reaches of our nearest orbit. Since it's a gas giant with an extremely solid core, it crushes the asteroids into atoms with its enormous pressure(if they weren't dissolved already by its acidic inner atmosphere). It is also considered to be a potential resource source, even though humanity lacks the technology and adequate spacecraft to mine them. Jupiter has the size of 1000 Earths(11 of them across the equator).
